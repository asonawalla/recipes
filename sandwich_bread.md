# Sandwich Bread

https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=lipLAgZkWN0

Wet Ingredients

* 175g water
* 125g milk
* 9g active dry yeast

Dry Ingredients

* 440g bread flour
* 8g fine sea salt
* 21g sugar

Other

* 3 tbsp butter

Procedure

* Kitchen aid all the ingredients
* 1 Hour rise
* Greased loaf plan, rolled dough
* 45 min - 1 Hour second rise
* Bake at 350 for 35-40 minutes
